How to enter?

In order to participate in the contest, each competitor will have to upload the track he wants to be evaluated on the Mixcloud platform. Each set must have a duration between 25 and 35 minutes. Any piece deemed valid may be inserted, including unreleased and/or self-produced tracks, including one's own singles. Together with the mix, each user will have to upload the tracklist of the set to Mixcloud with the hashtag #AMFCONTEST22
